About OneDash

Built at the pharmacy counter. Scaled for the health plan.

OneDash started with a narrow problem: the friction buried in everyday retail pharmacy processes. We built software to break those barriers down — and almost immediately saw that the same engine could do far more than we'd set out to solve. The plans sitting above those pharmacies were wrestling with the same problems at a much larger scale, and no one was giving them a way to see and act on it in real time.

Over the past three years, that realization has grown into a clear vision: a single dashboard that doesn't just show health plans where their gaps are, but closes them while they still count. Built on daily data and automated action, OneDash takes on the work plans have always thrown people at — quality gaps, spend gaps, the workflows that quietly eat a team's year — so results stop depending on how many hands a plan can put on the problem. That's the dashboard we're building:

one place where health plans get more done, with less scramble, and more certainty about what it's worth.
