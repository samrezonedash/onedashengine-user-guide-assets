About OneDash

Built at the pharmacy counter. Scaled for the health plan.

OneDash started at the pharmacy counter, automating the friction buried in everyday retail pharmacy work. We quickly saw the same engine could do far more — the health plans above those pharmacies faced the same problems at a much larger scale, with no way to see or act on them in real time.

That clinical grounding is now what powers the automation: OneDash doesn't just show plans where their gaps are, it closes them automatically while they still count. So results stop depending on how many hands you can put on the problem. That's what we're building:

one place where health plans get more done, with less scramble, and more certainty about what it's worth.
